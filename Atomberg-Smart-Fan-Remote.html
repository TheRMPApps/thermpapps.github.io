About Atomberg Smart Fan Remote

The Atomberg Smart Fan Remote app is designed to give you full control of your Atomberg ceiling fan directly from your smartphone. Forget the hassle of searching for the physical remote—now you can adjust speed, set timers, and switch modes instantly with a simple touch.

Key Features

  • Fan Control: Switch your fan on/off directly from your phone.
  • Speed Adjustment: Choose between multiple fan speeds for the perfect comfort level.
  • Timer Function: Schedule your fan to turn on or off automatically.
  • Modes: Access breeze mode, sleep mode, and normal operation.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Simple design for quick and easy use.

Why Use Atomberg Smart Fan Remote?

This app is perfect for those who want smart convenience when managing their ceiling fan. Whether you misplaced your remote or just want to control your fan via mobile, this app makes it effortless.

How to Use the App

Download the app, pair it with your Atomberg fan (compatible models), and start enjoying smart control instantly. Adjust fan speed, switch modes, or set timers with just a few taps on your smartphone.

Benefits

  • Full fan control without a physical remote.
  • Customizable speed and mode settings.
  • Energy-saving timer options.
  • Easy to use for all family members.
  • Convenient control from anywhere in range.
