WB Land Calculator

WB Land Calculator is a powerful and easy-to-use mobile app designed for residents of West Bengal to calculate land metrics, areas, and access property details quickly and accurately. Perfect for farmers, developers, students, and anyone dealing with land records.

Key Features

  • Land Measurement & Area Calculator: Supports multiple regional units like Acre, Bigha, Katha, Chatak, and Decimal for precise land measurements.
  • Area Unit Converter: Easily convert between different land units used across West Bengal.
  • Land Price Estimator: Helps estimate market value for land based on area and location.
  • Bilingual Support: Available in English and Bengali for wider accessibility.
  • Offline Functionality: Perform calculations without an internet connection.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Clean design with adjustable text size and theme options for easy use.

Why Choose WB Land Calculator?

WB Land Calculator simplifies the process of calculating and understanding land records in West Bengal. It reduces manual errors, saves time, and provides instant results for better planning and decision-making.
