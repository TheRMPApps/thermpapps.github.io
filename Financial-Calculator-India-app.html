Comprehensive Financial Tools Tailored for India

Financial Calculator India is a free app designed to simplify financial planning for Indian users. It offers a wide range of calculators covering various financial schemes and tools.

Key Features

  • EMI Calculator: Calculate Equated Monthly Installments for loans.
  • Investment Calculators: Tools for FD, RD, PPF, SIP, and more.
  • Tax Calculators: Income Tax and Capital Gains Tax calculators.
  • Insurance Calculators: PLI, RPLI, PMSBY, and PMJJBY calculators.
  • Government Schemes: NPS, EPF, VPF, and more.
  • Additional Tools: GST, Gold Price, Inflation, and more.
